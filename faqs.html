Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to water my plants?

The best way to water your plants is to check the soil moisture. Water when the top inch of soil is dry.

How often should I fertilize my plants?

Fertilize your plants every 4-6 weeks during the growing season and reduce during dormancy.

What are the signs of overwatering?

Signs of overwatering include yellowing leaves, wilting, and root rot.

How do I know if my plant is getting enough light?

If your plant is stretching towards the light or has leggy growth, it may not be getting enough light.
